    Use platforms like Livspace, MagicBricks, or UrbanClap. Check portfolios, reviews, and schedule consultations.
  • 3. How much does a 2BHK interior cost in Hyderabad?
    Typically ranges from ₹2.5L to ₹8L depending on customization and finishes.
  • 4. How much does 3BHK interior design cost in Hyderabad?
    Expect ₹4L to ₹12L based on modular fittings, furniture, and decor.
  • 5. Why is Livspace perfect for your home interior design?
    Livspace offers end-to-end solutions, transparent pricing, and professional project management.
  • 6. What will be the timelines for my project completion?
    Most interior projects take 45–90 days depending on complexity and material availability.
  • 7. What services are included under home interior design?
